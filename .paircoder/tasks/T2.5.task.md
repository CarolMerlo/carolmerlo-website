# Objective

Configure Netlify to email contact form submissions to `hwhcarol@gmail.com`. This is a dashboard-only change — no code required.

# Implementation Plan

Steps in the Netlify dashboard (app.netlify.com):

1. Log into Netlify
2. Select the carolmerlo.com site
3. Go to **Forms** in the left sidebar
4. Click on **contact** (the form name)
5. Click **Form notifications**
6. Click **Add notification** → **Email notification**
7. Enter `hwhcarol@gmail.com`
8. Click **Save**
9. Test: submit the contact form on the live site with a real message
10. Confirm email arrives at hwhcarol@gmail.com within a few minutes
