Books by Carol Merlo

Five Books. One Throughline.

Everything Carol writes comes back to the same question: what is actually in the way — and what becomes possible when you clear it? These books are practical tools for the inner and outer work of building a life and business you want to live in.

Transformation & Inner Work

The FIRE Formula

The FIRE Formula is the organizing framework behind all of Carol's coaching work. This book breaks it down: Focus, Ignition, Relationships, Execution — four dimensions that, when aligned, produce results that are sustainable rather than exhausting. Practical, honest, and built for real life.

Buy on Amazon

Taking the Mystery Out of Meditation

Meditation doesn't have to be complicated. This guide strips away the mysticism and gives you a clear, approachable way to start — and stick with — a practice that actually works. Written for people who are curious but skeptical, and for those who have tried and stopped.

Buy on Amazon

Create a Happy Life

Happiness is not an accident. This book explores the inner conditions — beliefs, habits, relationships — that determine how much of life we actually let in, and gives you specific tools for shifting them. Grounded in psychology, spiritual principle, and years of working with real people.

Buy on Amazon

Entrepreneurship & Business

Enlightened Entrepreneurship

How to build a successful solopreneurship from the ground up. This is the companion book to Carol's 8-week coaching program — it walks through the full FIRE Formula in the context of building a conscious business: finding your focus, removing blocks to momentum, navigating relationships, and building operational habits that hold.

Buy on Amazon

The Networking Conversation

Most people find networking uncomfortable because they're approaching it wrong. This book reframes the conversation — from transaction to relationship — and gives you a practical framework for connecting with people in a way that feels natural, authentic, and effective. Essential reading for any entrepreneur who wants to grow through genuine connection.

Buy on Amazon
