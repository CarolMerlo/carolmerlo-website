1-on-1 Spiritual Coaching

Spiritual Coaching with Carol Merlo

Sometimes the most important work isn't about doing more — it's about clearing what's standing between you and the life you already know is possible.

A Different Kind of Support

As a licensed spiritual practitioner with 15+ years of experience and a background in psychology and education, Carol works as a witness — someone who can hold steady while you move through what's hard, uncertain, or heavy.

This is not therapy, and it's not cheerleading. It's a grounded, quiet presence that helps you access your own clarity. The tools are real. The work goes deep. And what you leave with belongs to you.

Carol holds a Master's in Education, has trained in the Science of Mind tradition, and integrates sound frequency technology and psychology into her practice. She serves clients in Dallas in person and worldwide via Zoom.

What Happens in a Session

Sessions are conversational, intuitive, and tailored to what's alive for you in that moment. Carol listens deeply, reflects what she hears, and guides you toward the root of what's showing up — not just the surface symptom.

The work may draw from Spiritual Mind Treatment (affirmative prayer), somatic awareness, NLP, and frequency-based tools, depending on what you need.

  • Dallas in-person clients — sessions may incorporate PEMF (pulsed electromagnetic field) technology and live sound frequencies for a deeper somatic experience
  • Remote clients (Zoom) — frequency recordings are used to support the same depth of inner work from anywhere in the world

Each session is private, confidential, and held in a space of unconditional positive regard.

This Work Is Right For You If

  • You feel stuck, heavy, or disconnected — and ready to move
  • Something in your life (relationship, health, work, purpose) isn't flowing and you sense the block is inside you
  • You've done therapy or self-help work and you're ready for something that goes to the spiritual root
  • You're navigating grief, loss, transition, or a period of uncertainty
  • You want a container that is both deeply spiritual and practically grounded
  • You're curious about what's possible when the inner noise quiets and you can finally hear yourself
